## Fast Personality & Hook Generator

Roll or pick 2-3 elements:

**Motivation / Goal** (d10)  
1. Greed / treasure  
2. Revenge  
3. Protect family/village  
4. Religious zeal  
5. Power / control  
6. Knowledge / forbidden lore  
7. Survival / escape  
8. Fame / glory  
9. Redemption  
10. Pure chaos / amusement

**Quirk / Tell** (d12)  
1. Always smiling (even when threatening)  
2. Whispers when angry  
3. Constantly counts coins  
4. Fidgets with holy symbol  
5. Refers to self in third person  
6. Hates eye contact  
7. Collects teeth/fingers  
8. Speaks in rhymes  
9. Terrified of water  
10. Obsessed with cleanliness  
11. Carries broken weapon as memento  
12. Laughs at inappropriate moments

**One-line Hook**  
- Owes a huge debt to the Zhentarim  
- Knows the location of the last dragon egg  
- Is secretly the lost heir  
- Has been blackmailed by the BBEG  
- Is the only survivor of a destroyed party
